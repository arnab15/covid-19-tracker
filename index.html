PRECAUTIONS

Lockdown means LOCKDOWN! Avoid going out unless absolutely necessary. Stay safe!

STAY AT HOME

Many people are staying home and practicing social distancing to avoid spreading the viral disease further

WASH YOUR HAND

Washing your hands often will help protect you from germs. If soap and water are not available, use an alcohol-based hand rub.

STAY HEALTHY

especially when someone is ill. Get plenty of sleep, be physically active, manage your stress, drink plenty of fluids, and eat nutritious food.

USE FACEMASK

Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing. Try to Maintain at least 1 metre distance between you and people coughing or sneezing.

MAINTAIN SOCIAL DISTANCING

Practice physical distancing by avoiding unnecessary travel and staying away from large groups of people.

AVOID TOUCHING YOUR FACE

Avoid touching your face. and Wash your hands regularly with soap and water, or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.
